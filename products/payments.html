Payments



The introduction of EMV has benefited many financial institutions today. In particular, the number of fraudulent transactions involving credit cards have reduced significantly with the use of chip-based credit cards. As EMV continues to become the global standard in the payments industry, we see an increasing number of banks migrating from the conventional magnetic stripe cards to the smart card technology.

At JLM Solutions, we offer a broad range of smart card products which are certified by Visa and MasterCard based on the global EMV specifications. Our products are tailored to provide banks with enhanced security and control, and they are available at a competitive price with high quality.
