Identification



One of the primary roles of the smart card is to provide a safe method to uniquely identify a person's identity. An identification (ID) card is used in many areas especially in the government sector where it is issued as a national ID card to the citizens of a country. It contains a chip that stores the identification number and particulars of a citizen. Some notable implementations of the national ID card are MyKad in Malaysia and SCOSTA in India where millions of identity cards have been issued.

At JLM Solutions, we provide a wide range of ID card products designed to address the unique requirements of identification in any environment. Our ID cards can also easily integrate with other systems to enable the needs of identification in a particular field.
