Telecommunication



The SIM card is the most common type of smart card used around the world. It is estimated that there are more than 3 billion mobile subscribers globally and this figure is expected to grow higher each year. Since the SIM card was first introduced, it has evolved together with other technologies in the GSM industry. Today, we are seeing many different types of SIM cards which are specifically developed and customized to the growing demands in the mobile industry.

At JLM Solutions, we offer a unique range of SIM card products that fully complies to the ETSI 3GPP standards. They are customizeable to match the specific requirements and specifications of a mobile network operator (MNO) or mobile virtual network operator (MVNO).
